Build an ASCX Skin

Prerequisites

  • A :term:`host` (super user) account to upload skins.

Steps

  1. Log into DNN with a super user (host) account. Super user privileges are required to upload skins, because skins can include executables. (An administrator account is sufficient to apply skins and containers to a site.)
  3. Test the skin on a webpage.
    1. Create a new web page.
    2. Edit the page settings and go to Advanced Settings.
    3. Page Skin: Select Host and choose one of the layout templates from your new skin.
    4. Click Update.
    5. View your page in a browser to verify that the new skin was applied correctly.
